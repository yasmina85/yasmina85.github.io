Welcome to Yasmin AlNoamany site!

CLIR postdoc fellow in software curation with a Computer Science background. Yasmin leads original research in the areas of software curation and reproducibility. She works closely with the Research Data Management to develop training modules for researchers across the campus to ensure the integrity and reproducibility of scholarly activity. She also has partnered with the California Digital Library on a project for understanding researcher needs and values in regards to software for shaping strategies for software preservation and citations.
